Battleship

Objective
Sink all of your opponent's hidden ships before they sink yours
Setup
  • Ships can be placed horizontally or vertically, but not diagonally.
  • Each player places their 5 ships on their grid (hidden from the opponent):
    • Carrier (5 spaces)
    • Battleship (4 spaces)
    • Cruiser (3 spaces)
    • Submarine (3 spaces)
    • Destroyer (2 spaces)
Gameplay
